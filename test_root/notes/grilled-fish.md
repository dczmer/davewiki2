# Grilled Fish

A brief guide to grilling fish for delicious smoky flavors.

## Best Fish for Grilling

Firm-fleshed fish work best on the grill. #salmon and #swordfish hold their shape well and develop beautiful char marks. #tuna steaks are perfect for grilling, developing a tasty crust while staying tender inside. #halibut and #marlin are also excellent choices for the grill.
