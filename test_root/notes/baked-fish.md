# Baked Fish

A gentle cooking method that preserves moisture and natural flavors.

## Best Fish for Baking

Most fish bake well, but #cod and #haddock are classics for baked preparations. #salmon fillets baked with herbs are a favorite. #trout baked whole with lemon and herbs is elegant and simple. #snapper and #bass are also excellent baked.
