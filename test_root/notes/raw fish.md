# Raw Fish

Preparations that showcase fish in its natural, uncooked state.

## Best Fish for Raw Preparations

Only the freshest, highest-quality fish should be eaten raw. #salmon is popular for poke and sashimi. #tuna, especially bluefin and yellowfin, is prized for sushi and tartare. #halibut has a delicate sweetness when served raw. #snapper and #flounder are traditional sashimi choices in Japanese cuisine.
