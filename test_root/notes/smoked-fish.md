# Smoked Fish

Ancient preservation technique that imparts distinctive wood-smoked flavors.

## Best Fish for Smoking

Fatty fish absorb smoke flavors beautifully. #salmon is the most popular smoked fish, available as lox or hot-smoked. #mackerel and #herring have strong flavors that stand up to smoking. #trout smoked whole is a delicacy. #sturgeon and #eel are prized smoked fish in many cuisines.
