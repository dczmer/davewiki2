# Poached Fish

A delicate cooking method using gentle simmering for tender, moist results.

## Best Fish for Poaching

Delicate fish shine when poached. #salmon poached in wine or court bouillon is elegant. #halibut and #cod remain incredibly tender when poached. #pike and #sturgeon are traditional poaching fish in European cuisine. #trout poached whole makes a beautiful presentation.
