# Fried Fish

Classic crispy preparation methods from light pan-frying to deep-fried favorites.

## Best Fish for Frying

White, flaky fish are ideal for frying. #cod is the traditional choice for fish and chips. #tilapia and #flounder have mild flavors that pair well with breading. #catfish is a southern favorite when fried. #perch and #walleye fry beautifully with a light coating.
